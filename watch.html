Where to see the game in the Seattle area

High School Basketball

High school basketball games are a great starting point to experience the energy and talent of young athletes. To find game schedules, check your local school district's website or the athletic department's social media channels.

College Basketball

College basketball, particularly at the NCAA Division I level, serves as a vital stepping stone for emerging talents aiming for the professional leagues. These games offer more than just entertainment; they provide invaluable learning experiences. Young players can benefit immensely from watching the tactical plays, teamwork, and individual skills displayed on the court.

Seattle University

Seattle University is a well-regarded institution located in the heart of Seattle, offering diverse academic and athletic programs.

Division: NCAA Division I

Conference: Western Athletic Conference (WAC)

Schedule: View Schedule

Location: Redhawk Center, Seattle, WA (and Climate Pledge Arena for select games)

University of Washington

Located in Seattle, the University of Washington has a rich history of both academic and athletic excellence, including its basketball program.

Division: NCAA Division I

Conference: Pac-12 (moving to BIG 10 in 2024)

Schedule: View Schedule

Location: Alaska Airlines Arena, Seattle, WA

Professional Basketball

Professional basketball in Seattle is a topic tinged with nostalgia and hope, mainly due to the beloved Seattle SuperSonics. In the meantime, the Seattle Storm has been holding down the fort exceptionally well, garnering multiple championships. For fans seeking more live action, minor leagues like The Basketball League (TBL) offer a high level of play that's both entertaining and inspiring.

Seattle Storm

Founded in 2000, the Seattle Storm is a professional women's basketball team competing in the WNBA. They have a rich history, securing four championships in 2004, 2010, 2018, and 2020.

League: Women's National Basketball Association (WNBA)

Championships: 4 (2004, 2010, 2018, 2020)

Schedule: View Schedule

Location: Climate Pledge Arena

Seattle SuperHawks

The Seattle SuperHawks are a professional basketball team competing in The Basketball League (TBL). TBL is a minor league providing opportunities for players to develop their skills and gain exposure.

League: The Basketball League (TBL)

Schedule: View Schedule

Location: Seattle Pacific University - Royal Brougham Pavilion
