Beginner, Recreational and Development Basketball Programs

Basketball skill development is diverse, catering to players varied needs. Recreational leagues focus on game play, instilling a passion for the sport. Some leagues blend this with training sessions guided by volunteer coaches, introducing structured learning alongside competitive play. For targeted skill enhancement, group training classes are available, emphasizing specific basketball techniques. Meanwhile, small group sessions offer focused training with a closer coach-to-player interaction. And for those seeking specialized attention, private coaching delivers personalized lessons to refine individual game aspects. Each program type offers unique advantages, collectively presenting a comprehensive approach to basketball training.

Beginner Programs and Recreational Leagues

Seattle Parks and Recreation

Seattle Parks and Recreation oversees numerous community centers throughout the greater Seattle area. These centers play a pivotal role in fostering community engagement and providing recreational activities for all age groups, including kids.

  • Beginner Clinics: Tailored specifically for children new to basketball, these clinics focus on teaching the basic rules of the game, fundamental skills like dribbling, passing, and shooting, and emphasizing the importance of teamwork.
  • Youth Leagues: While these are generally for kids who have some experience, there are usually divisions or tiers within the leagues that cater to those just starting out. They offer a mix of practice sessions and actual games.
  • Drop-in Sessions: Some community centers might have specific hours dedicated to basketball, allowing kids to drop in and practice or play informally. This is a great way for kids to socialize and play in a low-pressure environment.
Visit Seattle Parks and Recreation

YMCA of Greater Seattle

Nationwide community organization offering basketball programs for kids. Programs include Rookie Basketball, Youth Basketball Leagues, and Skills Clinics.

  • Rookie Basketball: Designed for the youngest players and beginners, this program introduces kids to basketball in a fun, safe, and supportive environment. The emphasis here is on learning and enjoyment rather than competition.
  • Youth Basketball Leagues: These leagues are segmented by age and skill level. Even within the league structure, there are usually beginner tiers or divisions that focus on skill development, sportsmanship, and the basics of gameplay.
  • Skills Clinics: Regular clinics that focus on specific basketball skills. For beginners, these can be an excellent way to receive targeted training on foundational basketball skills.
Visit YMCA of Greater Seattle

Boys & Girls Clubs of King County

The Boys & Girls Clubs are known for their commitment to youth development through various programs, including sports. Their basketball programs are designed to be inclusive and developmental.

  • Intro to Basketball: A program specifically for younger kids and beginners. The focus is on basic skills, understanding the game, and most importantly, having fun.
  • Youth Leagues: While they cater to various skill levels, beginners are welcome, and there are often specific teams or divisions for those just starting out.
Visit Boys & Girls Clubs of King County

Skills Development Programs

Watts Basketball

 1644 Occidental Ave S Seattle, WA 98134

Watts Basketball offers player development based on an athlete’s level so each player continually feels challenged without feeling overwhelmed. The classes are designed to fit athletes' needs for optimum growth. They provide various programs for different age groups, from kindergarten to 12th grade. They also offer private and small group training, camps, and a special Watts Academy. They focus on not just winning games but also on the development of the players. They aim to empower personal growth through basketball skills. They also offer summer and winter camps.

  • Varsity & College Prep Training: This program is for 8th to 12th graders. It offers maximum value to committed, experienced players. Players are pushed towards their goals, evaluated, put in competition, and get individual skill work from elite top instructors.
  • Skills Training: This program is for 4th to 7th graders. It is designed for players focused on mastering the fundamentals and building a love for the game.
  • Little Dribblers: This program is for kindergarten to 3rd graders. It aims to help kids strengthen their foundation by improving their coordination and social skills, while building their love and interest for the game.
  • The Watts Academy: The academy aims to keep joy and development in the forefront of the player’s basketball experience. It uses practices, training sessions, and competitions as a means to accelerate basketball development and teach valuable life skills.
  • Camps: They offer various camps including School’s Out Camps for 3rd to 8th graders, Art of Shooting Camps for 7th to 12th graders, and Varsity Prep Camps for 8th to 11th graders.
Visit Watts Basketball

CTB Basketball

 Sammamish, WA

CTB Basketball provides values-based basketball skills-training for boys and girls grades 3 through 12. There is also the Eastside Basketball League, which is designed for athletes in the 4th to 8th grades with skills between the select and recreation levels who are looking for a place to play that will make them better.

  • Focus on Skill Development: The membership gives you access to the CTB Online Library with courses like our 20 Day Ball Handling Challenge, How to Learn a Left-Hand Layup in 30 Days, our Vertical Jump Program, and much more.
Visit CTB Basketball

Pinnacle Hoops

 15327 140th Pl NE, Woodinville, WA 98072

Pinnacle Hoops is a basketball training organization founded by professional player and Guinness World Record holder, Ashley Graham. They provide individual basketball skills training, team workouts, as well as clinics and camps in the Greater Seattle area. The mission of Pinnacle Hoops is to build confidence in young athletes through the sport of basketball and to push them to perform at their highest level by focusing on mental and physical preparation throughout their trainings, camps, and clinics.

  • Training Philosophy: The goal of their training sessions is to push players to perform at their highest level each time they step on the court. Their primary focus is developing basic fundamentals such as footwork, body mechanics and positioning. As players improve their fundamentals, their next target is to increase the player’s understanding and knowledge of the game.
  • Training Location: Ashley trains out of the Gold Creek Tennis & Sports Club located at 15327 140th Pl NE Woodinville, WA 98072. She can also commute to any gym in the Greater Seattle area providing gym space is available.
  • Camps and Clinics: They offer various camps and clinics such as the Complete Player Camp, Summer Kick-Off Ball-Handling Clinic, Scoring Camp and Offensive Skills Camp. They also offer a unique Benefit Clinic that directly benefits girls and women basketball players in another country.
  • Pinnacle Hoops International: Pinnacle Hoops International is a nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing the sport of basketball to youth and women around the world. They offer free basketball clinics and camps in communities that may lack the infrastructure or equipment for players to compete at a high level.
Visit Pinnacle Hoops

Skill Sets and Band Reps

 Seattle, WA

Skill Sets and Band Reps is a basketball training program that uses the r3bartraining methodology to help players focus on intentional form, control, tempo, pace, and routine awareness. The program offers basketball skillset development, r3bar training, yoga, and private training. The program is designed to enhance players' skills, develop their strength, and elevate their game.

  • Basketball Skillset Development: The program focuses on developing athletes’ basketball skills and physical strength using the R3bartraining methodology. They offer group classes and private sessions.
  • R3Bar Training: R3Bar is a functional basketball strength & conditioning equipment adopted by many of the NBA's brightest stars & athletes nationwide. It is a portable Exercise Training Tool designed for Athletic Performance Enhancement, Pre-habilitation, Rehabilitation, Therapeutic & Active Recovery and for Improving overall freedom of mobility and movement.
  • Yoga: The program includes yoga sessions as part of their training methodology.
  • Private Training: They offer private basketball skill development sessions for more focused and personalized training.
  • Pricing: Basketball Skill Development Class costs $75 with a capacity of 10+. Private Basketball Skill Development Session costs $150. They also offer a package of 10 Basketball Skill Development Sessions for $700 and a Monthly Unlimited package for $1,000.
  • Team: The team includes Michael Knight, Tim Manson, and Ryan Cooper, all of whom have extensive experience and expertise in basketball training and athletic performance enhancement.
Visit Skill Sets and Band Reps
